How To Monetize My Crime and Safety Intelligence Platform

I plan to create a website where users can check for the most dangerous areas, the places with the most security, the areas with the highest crime rate and so on, this is a table on ways to monetize it

The platform will be called SecureStreet

# Monetization Path Income Type Target Audience How It Works Example of What You Sell
1️⃣ Freemium + Paid Tiers Subscription Students, Residents, Parents, Travelers Offer basic crime maps and alerts for free, while charging users for access to advanced tools like personalized alerts, safe route planning, and offline access. - Daily safety notifications
- Safe route planner
- Crime heatmaps
- Emergency contact alerts
- Housing safety scores
2️⃣ Data-as-a-Service (DaaS) B2B Data Licensing Logistics Firms, Real Estate Companies, NGOs, Fintechs Provide structured crime and safety data in formats like CSVs, reports, or dashboards. Businesses pay to use this data in logistics, planning, research, or pricing strategies. - Crime trend reports (monthly/quarterly)
- Heatmap data exports
- Neighborhood safety ranking files
- Zone comparison dashboards
3️⃣ API Licensing Developer Platform (SaaS) Tech Startups, Real Estate Sites, Ride Apps, Travel Platforms You expose your intelligence engine via API. External platforms pay to use your data (like Google Maps, but for crime/safety) directly inside their apps and services. - Safety Score API
- Safe Route API
- Real-time Area Alerts API
- Top Dangerous Zones API
4️⃣ Security-Focused Ads Pay-Per-Click / Sponsorships Local Security Firms, CCTV Installers, Secure Estates Show targeted ads on the platform when users browse high-risk zones. Charge businesses per click, impression, or for premium listing placements. - Sponsored zones on map
- Ads from patrol services
- Safe estate promotions
- Home security installation offers
5️⃣ Housing Safety Partnership Property Promotions & Badging Landlords, Hostel Agents, Property Platforms, Tenants Verify and promote safe housing. Charge landlords or agents to get their properties badged as “Verified Safe” and featured on the platform. - Safe Housing Badge (₦)
- Tenant safety reports
- Verified listings section
- Housing risk map overlays
6️⃣ Tourist & Student Safety Packs One-Time Product Sales Tourists, NYSC, Students, Parents Sell downloadable maps, safety guides, hostel reports, and offline tools like panic alerts or safe route planners. Ideal for first-time buyers and one-time users. - “Safe in Lagos” digital guide
- Offline crime zone map
- Walking risk scanner
- “Track Me” safety alert tool
