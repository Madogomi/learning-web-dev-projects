Learning to code

I will upload videos and playlists that i watched while learning full stack website development.

This is a step by step guide.

But, DO NOT JUST WATCH THIS VIDEOS, actually go out there and build, play around with code, break things, fix them, grow, google for how to do stuff that you don't know or understand. We even have stuff like ChatGPT, Claude AI and Gemini and other AIs to help and assist you on your learning journey.

But, BEWARE: DO NOT LET CHATGPT AND ANY OTHER AI BECOME A CRUTCH YOU CAN'T LIVE WITHOUT, WRITE THE CODE YOURSELF, DON'T JUST COPY THE CODE AND SAY IT'S FINE BECAUSE YOU UNDERSTAND THE CODE, WRITE THE CODE YOURSELF, PLAY AROUND WITH IT, CHECK OUT WHAT WORKS AND WHAT DOESN'T WORK.

NOTE: You don't have to watch the full playlists or the full videos, they are just guides, the main thing is to practice, i'm only putting the videos so that you will watch it and understand where to start.
